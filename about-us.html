Welcome to In Their World

Mission

In their World’s mission is to provide home-based services for children and families affected by autism spectrum disorder (ASD), and related conditions. We promote support, educate, advocate and love. We value your trust and are here to honor your family wishes, as we take this journey together with you, and your family.

Vission

In Their World, envisions a safe environment in which individuals and families living with autism, and related conditions can improve the quality of life and receive the highest level of dignity, and live in a society in which their, talents and abilities are valued and appreciated.

We can achieve this together through Advocacy, Support, Educate and Love.​ ​We are committed to:

Increasing global understanding & acceptance of people with autism and related conditions.

Help them interact with others

Learn and play

Build independence

Focus on goals that lead to an improvement in a person’s quality of life

Have their wishes honored in family, school and their community.
